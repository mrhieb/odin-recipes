Rice Bowl

There are many ways to make rice bowls. I like to use as many veggies as I can.

Feel free to modify the ingredients as you see fit

Recipe

Ingredients:

    Rice
    Carrots
    Green Onions
    Cucmbers
    Spinach
    Black Beans
    Avacado

Prep:

Cut up all veggies into small sections.

Process:

Cook Rice. Cook beans if you are using dried beans. Add rice to individual bowls. Add veggies on top of rice. You can add any sauce that you would like on top of this. It could be a salad dressing or hot sauce or soy sauce. Enjoy!!

Oscar Pasta

My son loves pasta, but he likes really simple food

This is his favorite meal.

Recipe

Ingredients:

    Dry Pasta
    Butter
    Salt
    Spices: Thyme

Prep:

Nothing

Process:

Heat water until boiling. Add pasta. After 10-12 minutes, strain the pasta. Turn the heat to low and return the pasta to the pot. Add butter and thyme and mix together.

Rice and Beans

There are many ways to make beans and rice. I usually change my recipe depending upon what leftover I have.

Feel free to modify the ingredients as you see fit

Recipe

Ingredients:

    Beans
    Rice
    Garlic
    Onions
    Diced tomatoes and chilis(can)
    Spices: Chili powder, Oregano, Cumin, Salt

Prep:

Use a garlic press to dice 2-3 large galic cloves. Dice an onion. Rinse beans if bought in a can.

Process:

I heat up about 3 Tablespoons of Olive oil and add the garlic and onion. I stir this for about 3-5 minutes. Then, I add the black beans and the can of chili beans(without staining). Then, I add Rotel diced tomatoes and chilis. Cook until the beans become more tender. Add Salt, Chili powder, Oregano, and cumin to taste. I just throw a decent amount of salt(1 teaspoon), but ¼ teaspoon of oregano and cumin. I don’t add chili powder if I get chili beans. However, I add it if I use 2 cans of black beans.

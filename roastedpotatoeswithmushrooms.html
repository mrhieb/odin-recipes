Potatoes and Mushrooms

This is one of my favorite meals. There are many variations of this recipe.

This recipe is vegetarian, but you are free to add sausages if you eat meat.

Recipe

Ingredients:

    Mushrooms
    Potatoes
    Onions
    Siracha
    Olive Oil
    Salt
    Hearty Green Salad

Prep:

Dice an Onion. Cut potatoes into quarter inch pieces. Clean and quarter mushrooms.

Process:

Coat Potatoes in Olive oil and salt. Mix to make sure all potatoes are evenly coated. Put Potatoes in air fryer for 12 minutes.
Heat Oil on a pan and add mushrooms. Cook on medium heat until tender.
Make a good hearty salad such as Kale or Cole Slaw.
Combine all of the ingredients together and add siracha to the top.
